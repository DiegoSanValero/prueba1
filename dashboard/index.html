Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Which agreement applies to me?

In the legal area, Ground Coffee follows the agreement Convenio Colectivo Estatal de la Elaboración de Productos Cocinados para su Venta a Domicilio. In addition, if you have a fixed-term contract, the Modificación del Convenio Colectivo 2023 also applies to you.

ref. Agencia Estatal Boletín Oficial del Estado (BOE)

Am I entitled to vacation?

Yes, you are entitled to thirty-one calendar days of paid annual leave that can not be replaced by financial compensation.

ref. Artículo 13 del Convenio Colectivo

Am I entitled to leaves?

Yes, you are entitled to leaves. In addition to the leaves described in Estatuto de los Trabajadores, you will be able to take unpaid and recoverable leave of one day per year for personal matters.

ref. Artículo 37 of the Estatuto de los Trabajadores
ref. Artículo 4 del Convenio Colectivo

I have been harassed at work, what do I do?

Harassment at work is a form of violence that violates the dignity and integrity of people and you should not be tolerated or consent. If you have suffered or believe that you are suffering harassment at work, immediately inform your union representative or the company management.

Remember that you are not alone and you have the right to work in a respectful and safe environment.

ref. Instituto de las Mujeres (IMs)
ref. Hacienda y Administración Pública
